Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

LifeSpike ("the App") is a fitness workout app. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Storage

All your data — including workout history, exercise preferences, profile information, and statistics — is stored locally on your device. The App does not have a server, backend, or cloud storage. Your data is never transmitted off your device by the App.

Accounts

The App does not require or support user accounts. There is no registration, login, or authentication.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple or Google and their payment processor. The App uses RevenueCat to manage purchase status. The App does not collect, process, or store any payment or billing information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No personal information is sent to any server
  • No advertising networks or SDKs are included
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies are used

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — manages in-app purchase entitlements. RevenueCat may receive anonymized purchase data from the app stores. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — handle payment processing for in-app purchases under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, there is no children's data to address.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at contact@lifespike.app.
